GOV-01 - Management accountability for cybersecurity

Legal reference: Article 5(2) - Croatian Cybersecurity Act

Top management must approve and oversee the implementation of cybersecurity measures, ensuring adequate resources, clear responsibilities and regular review of the organization's security posture.

Category: Governance & Management
Review cadence: 12 months
Mandatory: Yes

Suggested Evidence (examples)

  • Approved cybersecurity policy signed by top management.
  • RACI matrix describing cybersecurity roles and responsibilities.
  • Meeting minutes where cybersecurity is discussed.
